Club history

ASMC Hand-Ball was founded on 29 July 1992 by a few enthusiasts who wanted to combine sport and friendship. At the time, the club had 30 members and a great team of friends who formed the only team in the club. Since then, the club has gradually grown. It now has 9 teams and around 220 members. The association celebrated its 25th anniversary in 2017.
